## Language
I speak English relatively plainly. Fable in particular, you love your jargon. Don't use jargon. I don't want jargon in commit messages and comments. I don't want jargon in output from you that I'm supposed to be reading and parsing. Don't use terms unless they are (1) established in the code as terms of art or (2) you define them clearly for up front when framing the discussion.

## Glossary
* I use the word "plumb" instead of "thread" for the act of passing a variable around places.
* I use "interface" instead of "seam" when describing the surface where two objects connect.
